    Men's Bomber Jacket

    The men's bomber jacket is a strong wardrobe must-have with a classic edge. From oversized varsity styles to longline lengths, the bomber is a key layering piece to reinvent each season.


    Structure your look with a sleek men's bomber jacket.


    Taking inspiration from vintage American aviation uniforms, the men's bomber jacket boasts a streamlined silhouette constructed from a soft padded base. Layer it over a hooded men's sweatshirt when the weather turns cooler, trapping in the heat with tonal layers as a chic alternative to an oversized coat. Channel your urban style by teaming a long length bomber jacket with a men's print t-shirt, playing with a balance of structure and pattern.


    Master the art of luxe layering with a men's bomber jacket.


    Team a jersey men's bomber jacket with a pair of men's loose fit jeans for a sleek off-duty look, teaming with a pair of chunky trainers to maximise on comfort. Choose a bright colour to make a statement when you're on the go, opting for tonal accessories to let the vibrant hues do the talking. Alternatively, go for a quilted bomber jacket for a unique twist, teaming with a patterned scarf and polo shirt to complete the preppy style.
