Go Away, I'm Reading Candle


Ever wish you could be left alone to finish that last chapter or last page? Let this candle help you remain undisturbed with a yummy blend of Orange Scones, Cinnamon Buns, and Cranberry Muffins!



4 oz candle in silver metal tin with lid
Burn Time is 30+ hours if burned correctly

8 oz candle in 9 oz glass jar with black metal lid
Burn Time is 50+ hours if burned correctly


Made from all-natural soy wax that allows for a strong scent throw and easy clean up. Most fragrance oils are Phthalate free. Wicks are 100% cotton and contain no zinc.


Safety Notes:
Never leave a burning candle unattended.
Always keep wicks trimmed to 1/4 of an inch.
Never burn for more than 4 hours at a time.

Each one of my fragrances are mixed by me and are not available anywhere else!

Be sure to check out my shop, as I have over 100 different books and characters! Also, follow my shop for updates, as I am always releasing new candles and scents.

All melts & candles are made to order, and hand poured by me in my kitchen. I try to keep everything in stock, but please allow up to 2 weeks for processing, and all orders ship USPS from Simi Valley, CA.
