DESIGN – one of the best hits in the bag world. Originally dating back to the 19th century, now refurbished for modern use. You don’t have to own a stethoscope to carry it: make it your everyday bag to the office, take it on a trip. Show off your personality a little. Complemented by charisma.

STORY – a Time Resistance bag is not an accessory of the season, but a companion for life. Its skin is an empty canvas, that will get scarred throughout the years, and that’s what will make it truly beautiful and unique -- the marks from your life and experiences that tell your personal story.

MATERIALS
• Exterior made from genuine full-grain calfskin that will last you for decades.
• Interior fully lined with cotton and easy to clean.

TAN

• This artisan technique maintains the natural look of leather, rendering each skin one of a kind with a slightly different pattern.
• As an added bonus, vegetable tanning gives it a natural earthy smell.
